Company Overview

Strengthening Agriculture, Empowering Growth

Agri Biochem Pvt. Ltd. (ABCPL) is a professionally organized public limited company headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Since our incorporation on 26th February 2020 under the Company Incorporation Rules, 2014, we have been dedicated to manufacturing nearly 100 high-quality Insecticides, Pesticides, and Fungicides, ensuring availability across the country.

In a short span of time, ABCPL has gained recognition both as a toll manufacturer and a contract manufacturer, serving diverse agricultural needs. Today, we proudly stand as one of the fastest-growing ISO 9001:2015 certified Agro-Chemical companies in India.

Our journey is powered by an integrated approach — from Research to Manufacturing and Marketing — allowing us to provide innovative solutions for modern farming. With a comprehensive vision of agribusiness, ABCPL is committed to meeting the rising demands of global agriculture and contributing to a more sustainable future.

Our Objectives

Driving Innovation Through Quality & Research

At Agri Biochem Pvt. Ltd., achieving and sustaining competence in quality control is our primary quality objective. We leverage our experience and expertise to bring a scientific edge to our products and services, ensuring consistent performance and customer satisfaction.

Our long-term objectives include:

  • Developing analytical skills among employees
  • Improving ingredient quality and product safety
  • Building a database for ectoparasites and pathogens
  • Manufacturing solutions to eliminate and prevent pathogens
  • Supplying cutting-edge pest control products
  • Providing continuous employee training
  • Innovating in insect farming and pest management
  • Addressing warehouse pest challenges effectively

Customer feedback and satisfaction remain the driving force behind our innovations. With a strong commitment to principles and research & development, ABCPL continues to set benchmarks in the agro-chemical industry.
